The Philosophy

Dependency is the enemy. Cloud platforms disappear. APIs get deprecated. Services shut down. The only infrastructure you truly own is the one you build yourself. That's why the RTX 4060 runs local AI inference, not cloud calls. That's why the OS is self-hosted. That's why every tool is custom-built.

Split-second decisions in-game translate directly to split-second decisions on a live chart. The discipline is the same.
